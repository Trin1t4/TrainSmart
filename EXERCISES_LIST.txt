## LOWER PUSH (10)

### 1. Bodyweight Squat
**Setup:** Feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out, arms extended forward
**Cues:**
- Push hips back first, then bend knees
- Keep chest up, eyes forward
- Knees track over toes
- Descend until thighs parallel to floor
- Drive through heels to stand
**Avoid:** Knees caving in, heels lifting, excessive forward lean

---

### 2. Goblet Squat
**Setup:** Hold dumbbell/kettlebell at chest level, elbows pointing down, feet shoulder-width
**Cues:**
- Keep weight close to chest
- Elbows inside knees at bottom
- Chest stays tall throughout
- Push knees out as you descend
- Full depth - hips below knees
**Avoid:** Letting weight pull you forward, rounding upper back

---

### 3. Front Squat
**Setup:** Barbell on front delts, elbows high, fingertip grip or crossed arms
**Cues:**
- Elbows up - parallel to floor
- Torso stays vertical
- Break at hips and knees together
- Drive knees forward over toes
- Breathe into core, brace hard
**Avoid:** Elbows dropping, forward lean, wrists bending back

---

### 4. Back Squat
**Setup:** Bar on upper traps (high bar) or rear delts (low bar), hands outside shoulders
**Cues:**
- Big breath, brace core
- Unrack with both feet under bar
- Hips back and down together
- Chest up, neutral spine
- Drive through mid-foot to stand
**Avoid:** Butt wink at bottom, knees caving, good-morning the weight up

---

### 5. Leg Press
**Setup:** Back flat against pad, feet hip-width on platform, knees at 90°
**Cues:**
- Lower back stays pressed into pad
- Control the descent - 2-3 seconds
- Don't lock knees at top
- Push through whole foot
- Full range of motion
**Avoid:** Letting hips lift off pad, bouncing at bottom, locking knees

---

### 6. Bulgarian Split Squat
**Setup:** Rear foot on bench (laces down), front foot 2 steps ahead
**Cues:**
- Torso slightly forward
- Drop back knee straight down
- Front knee tracks over toes
- Drive through front heel
- Keep hips square
**Avoid:** Front knee too far forward, twisting hips, rear foot too high

---

### 7. Pistol Squat
**Setup:** Stand on one leg, other leg extended forward, arms out for balance
**Cues:**
- Sit back and down slowly
- Keep extended leg off floor
- Heel stays planted
- Full depth - hamstring to calf
- Drive up without momentum
**Avoid:** Collapsing knee inward, losing balance, using momentum

---

### 8. Lunges
**Setup:** Standing tall, feet hip-width, hands on hips or at sides
**Cues:**
- Step forward, land heel first
- Both knees at 90° at bottom
- Back knee hovers above floor
- Torso stays upright
- Push back to start through front heel
**Avoid:** Knee going past toes, short steps, leaning forward

---

### 9. Step-up
**Setup:** Face box/bench at knee height, one foot fully on platform
**Cues:**
- Drive through top foot only
- Don't push off back foot
- Stand fully upright at top
- Control the descent
- Keep chest tall
**Avoid:** Bouncing off back leg, leaning forward, too high box

---

### 10. Leg Extension
**Setup:** Sit with back against pad, ankles behind roller, knees at edge of seat
**Cues:**
- Extend legs fully
- Squeeze quads at top - pause
- Control the negative - 2-3 seconds
- Don't swing or use momentum
- Keep back flat against pad
**Avoid:** Lifting hips, using momentum, hyperextending knees

---

## LOWER PULL (9)

### 11. Bodyweight Hip Hinge
**Setup:** Feet hip-width, slight knee bend, hands on thighs or behind head
**Cues:**
- Push hips straight back
- Slide hands down thighs (if used)
- Feel hamstring stretch
- Spine stays neutral
- Squeeze glutes to return
**Avoid:** Rounding lower back, bending knees too much, looking up

---

### 12. Conventional Deadlift
**Setup:** Feet hip-width, bar over mid-foot, hands just outside legs
**Cues:**
- Push floor away with legs
- Bar stays close to body
- Hips and shoulders rise together
- Lock out with glutes, not lower back
- Reverse the movement down
**Avoid:** Rounding back, bar drifting forward, jerking the weight

---

### 13. Romanian Deadlift (RDL)
**Setup:** Hold bar at hips, feet hip-width, slight knee bend
**Cues:**
- Push hips back, not down
- Bar slides down thighs
- Stop when you feel hamstring stretch
- Neutral spine throughout
- Drive hips forward to stand
**Avoid:** Bending knees too much, rounding back, going too low

---

### 14. Sumo Deadlift
**Setup:** Wide stance, toes out 45°, hands inside knees on bar
**Cues:**
- Push knees out over toes
- Chest up, hips down
- Drive through heels
- Keep bar close to body
- Lock out hips and knees together
**Avoid:** Knees caving, hips shooting up first, rounded back

---

### 15. Good Morning
**Setup:** Bar on upper back, feet shoulder-width, slight knee bend
**Cues:**
- Hinge at hips, not waist
- Push butt back
- Torso almost parallel to floor
- Feel hamstring stretch
- Squeeze glutes to return
**Avoid:** Rounding spine, bending knees too much, going too fast

---

### 16. Hip Thrust
**Setup:** Upper back on bench, feet flat, bar in hip crease
**Cues:**
- Drive through heels
- Squeeze glutes hard at top
- Full hip extension - hips in line with knees and shoulders
- Chin tucked, look forward
- Control the descent
**Avoid:** Hyperextending lower back, pushing through toes, incomplete lockout

---

### 17. Glute Bridge
**Setup:** Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat, arms at sides
**Cues:**
- Push through heels
- Lift hips until body is straight
- Squeeze glutes at top - pause
- Don't arch lower back
- Lower with control
**Avoid:** Using lower back, feet too far from glutes, rushing

---

### 18. Nordic Curl
**Setup:** Kneel on pad, ankles secured, torso upright, arms ready to catch
**Cues:**
- Lower slowly - fight gravity
- Keep hips extended
- Body stays straight
- Lower as far as you can control
- Push back up (or use hands)
**Avoid:** Bending at hips, dropping too fast, losing control

---

### 19. Leg Curl
**Setup:** Lie face down, ankles under pad, knees just off bench edge
**Cues:**
- Curl heels toward glutes
- Squeeze hamstrings at top
- Control the negative
- Keep hips pressed down
- Don't lift thighs off bench
**Avoid:** Using momentum, lifting hips, incomplete range

---

## UPPER PUSH HORIZONTAL (7)

### 20. Push-up
**Setup:** Hands under shoulders, body straight from head to heels, core tight
**Cues:**
- Lower chest to floor
- Elbows at 45° angle
- Full range of motion
- Push back to start
- Core stays engaged
**Avoid:** Sagging hips, flaring elbows 90°, partial reps

---

### 21. Bench Press
**Setup:** Feet flat, back arched, shoulder blades pinched, grip slightly wider than shoulders
**Cues:**
- Unrack with straight arms
- Lower to lower chest/sternum
- Elbows at 45-75° angle
- Drive through feet
- Press up and slightly back
**Avoid:** Bouncing off chest, flaring elbows, lifting butt off bench

---

### 22. Dumbbell Bench Press
**Setup:** Dumbbells at shoulders, palms forward, feet flat, back arched
**Cues:**
- Press up and together
- Lower with control
- Elbows at 45° angle
- Full stretch at bottom
- Squeeze chest at top
**Avoid:** Dumbbells drifting, bouncing, losing arch

---

### 23. Incline Push-up
**Setup:** Hands on elevated surface, body straight, arms extended
**Cues:**
- Lower chest to surface
- Keep core tight
- Full range of motion
- Elbows at 45°
- Push back to start
**Avoid:** Sagging hips, neck strain, partial reps

---

### 24. Decline Push-up
**Setup:** Feet elevated on bench/box, hands on floor, body straight
**Cues:**
- Lower chest to floor
- Keep hips in line
- Elbows at 45°
- Core stays tight
- Push up with control
**Avoid:** Pike at hips, neck straining up, sagging middle

---

### 25. Diamond Push-up
**Setup:** Hands together forming diamond shape, thumbs and index fingers touching
**Cues:**
- Elbows stay close to body
- Lower chest to hands
- Keep core tight
- Full range of motion
- Press up through triceps
**Avoid:** Flaring elbows out, partial reps, sagging hips

---

### 26. Dips
**Setup:** Grip bars, arms straight, shoulders down, slight forward lean
**Cues:**
- Lower until upper arms parallel
- Elbows back, not out
- Forward lean for chest
- Press up to lockout
- Control the movement
**Avoid:** Going too deep, excessive forward lean, swinging

---

## UPPER PUSH VERTICAL (7)

### 27. Pike Push-up
**Setup:** Hands and feet on floor, hips high, forming inverted V
**Cues:**
- Lower head toward floor
- Elbows bend to sides
- Head goes between hands
- Push up through shoulders
- Keep legs straight
**Avoid:** Bending knees, insufficient pike, incomplete ROM

---

### 28. Handstand Push-up
**Setup:** Kick up to wall, hands shoulder-width, fingers spread, core tight
**Cues:**
- Lower head to floor
- Elbows go forward
- Press up through shoulders
- Stack joints - wrists, elbows, shoulders
- Control throughout
**Avoid:** Arching back, elbows flaring, kicking off wall

---

### 29. Overhead Press
**Setup:** Bar in front rack, feet hip-width, core braced, elbows slightly forward
**Cues:**
- Press bar straight up
- Move head back, then forward
- Lock out overhead
- Bar over mid-foot at top
- Lower with control
**Avoid:** Excessive back lean, pressing forward, loose core

---

### 30. Dumbbell Shoulder Press
**Setup:** Dumbbells at shoulders, palms forward, elbows under wrists
**Cues:**
- Press up and together
- Full lockout at top
- Lower with control
- Keep core tight
- No excessive lean
**Avoid:** Arching back, incomplete lockout, using legs

---

### 31. Arnold Press
**Setup:** Dumbbells at shoulders, palms facing you, elbows forward
**Cues:**
- Rotate palms as you press
- Full overhead extension
- Reverse rotation down
- Smooth, continuous motion
- Control the weight
**Avoid:** Rushing rotation, incomplete ROM, excessive lean

---

### 32. Lateral Raise
**Setup:** Dumbbells at sides, slight bend in elbows, feet shoulder-width
**Cues:**
- Raise arms to sides
- Stop at shoulder height
- Lead with elbows
- Slight forward lean
- Lower with control
**Avoid:** Swinging, going above shoulders, straight arms

---

### 33. Front Raise
**Setup:** Dumbbells in front of thighs, palms down or neutral
**Cues:**
- Raise arms to shoulder height
- Keep slight elbow bend
- One arm at a time or both
- Control the descent
- Core stays tight
**Avoid:** Swinging, going above eye level, using momentum

---

## UPPER PULL HORIZONTAL (6)

### 34. Inverted Row
**Setup:** Hang under bar, body straight, grip shoulder-width or wider
**Cues:**
- Pull chest to bar
- Squeeze shoulder blades
- Keep body straight
- Elbows at 45° angle
- Lower with control

### 35. Barbell Row

**Cues:**
- Pull bar to lower chest/upper abs
- Drive elbows back
- Squeeze at top
- Lower with control
- Torso stays stable
**Avoid:** Using momentum, rounding back, standing too upright

---

### 36. Dumbbell Row
**Setup:** One hand and knee on bench, other foot on floor, arm hanging
**Cues:**
- Pull elbow past torso
- Squeeze shoulder blade back
- Lower with stretch
- Keep back flat
- No torso rotation
**Avoid:** Twisting body, using momentum, incomplete ROM

---

### 37. Cable Row
**Setup:** Sit with feet on platform, knees slightly bent, grip handle
**Cues:**
- Pull handle to lower chest
- Drive elbows back
- Squeeze shoulder blades
- Return with control
- Torso stays upright
**Avoid:** Leaning back too far, rounding forward, using momentum

---

### 38. T-Bar Row
**Setup:** Straddle bar, chest on pad or hinged over, grip handles
**Cues:**
- Pull elbows past body
- Squeeze back at top
- Lower with stretch
- Keep chest up
- Stable torso
**Avoid:** Jerking weight, excessive body English, rounding back

---

### 39. Face Pull
**Setup:** Cable at face height, rope attachment, step back
**Cues:**
- Pull to face/forehead
- Separate rope at end
- External rotate shoulders
- Squeeze rear delts
- Control return
**Avoid:** Pulling too low, using too much weight, no rotation

## UPPER PULL VERTICAL (4)

### 40. Pull-up
**Setup:** Dead hang, hands outside shoulders, palms away
**Cues:**
- Initiate with shoulder blades
- Pull chest to bar
- Chin over bar
- Lower to full hang
- Control the negative
**Avoid:** Kipping, partial reps, neck straining forward

---

### 41. Chin-up
**Setup:** Dead hang, hands shoulder-width, palms facing you
**Cues:**
- Pull chest to bar
- Chin over bar
- Squeeze biceps at top
- Full extension at bottom
- Control throughout
**Avoid:** Swinging, incomplete ROM, excessive kip

---

### 42. Lat Pulldown
**Setup:** Grip wide, sit with thighs under pads, slight lean back
**Cues:**
- Pull bar to upper chest
- Drive elbows down and back
- Squeeze lats at bottom
- Control the return
- Full stretch at top
**Avoid:** Pulling behind neck, leaning too far back, using momentum

---

### 43. Assisted Pull-up
**Setup:** Select assistance, grip bar, knees or feet on platform
**Cues:**
- Pull chin over bar
- Squeeze at top
- Lower with control
- Full extension at bottom
- Use minimal assistance
**Avoid:** Relying too much on assist, partial reps, swinging

## CORE (7)

### 44. Plank
**Setup:** Forearms on floor, elbows under shoulders, body straight
**Cues:**
- Squeeze glutes
- Brace core like punch coming
- Neutral spine
- Don't hold breath
- Push floor away
**Avoid:** Sagging hips, pike up, holding breath

---

### 45. Dead Bug
**Setup:** Lie on back, arms up, knees at 90°, low back pressed down
**Cues:**
- Extend opposite arm and leg
- Keep low back flat
- Exhale as you extend
- Return with control
- Alternate sides
**Avoid:** Arching back, holding breath, rushing

---

### 46. Bird Dog
**Setup:** Hands under shoulders, knees under hips, neutral spine
**Cues:**
- Extend opposite arm and leg
- Keep hips level
- Squeeze glute at top
- Hold briefly
- Return with control
**Avoid:** Rotating hips, arching back, rushing

---

### 47. Hanging Leg Raise
**Setup:** Dead hang from bar, shoulders engaged, legs straight
**Cues:**
- Curl pelvis up
- Raise legs parallel or higher
- Control the descent
- Minimize swing
- Exhale on the lift
**Avoid:** Swinging, using momentum, incomplete ROM

---

### 48. Ab Wheel Rollout
**Setup:** Kneeling, grip wheel handles, arms extended
**Cues:**
- Roll forward with control
- Keep core tight
- Extend as far as controlled
- Pull back with abs
- Hips stay neutral
**Avoid:** Sagging hips, going too far, using hip flexors

---

### 49. Cable Crunch
**Setup:** Kneel facing cable, rope behind head, hips fixed
**Cues:**
- Crunch ribs to hips
- Round upper back
- Squeeze abs at bottom
- Don't pull with arms
- Control the return
**Avoid:** Using arms, moving hips, incomplete crunch

---

### 50. Pallof Press
**Setup:** Side to cable, hold handle at chest, feet shoulder-width
**Cues:**
- Press handle straight out
- Resist rotation
- Hold at extension
- Return to chest
- Core stays braced
**Avoid:** Rotating torso, rushing, using too much weight

## TRICEPS (3)

### 51. Tricep Dips
**Setup:** Hands on bench behind you, legs extended, hips close to bench
**Cues:**
- Lower until elbows at 90°
- Keep elbows pointing back
- Press up to lockout
- Don't flare elbows
- Control the movement
**Avoid:** Going too deep, elbows flaring, shrugging shoulders

---

### 52. Tricep Pushdown
**Setup:** Face cable, grip bar/rope, elbows at sides
**Cues:**
- Push down to full extension
- Squeeze triceps at bottom
- Only forearms move
- Control the return
- Elbows stay pinned
**Avoid:** Moving elbows, leaning over, using momentum

---

### 53. Skull Crushers
**Setup:** Lie on bench, arms extended, narrow grip on bar/dumbbells
**Cues:**
- Lower weight to forehead
- Keep upper arms still
- Extend to lockout
- Control the negative
- Elbows point to ceiling
**Avoid:** Flaring elbows, moving shoulders, bouncing

## PELVIC FLOOR - Post-Partum (11)

### 58. Connection Breath
**Setup:** Lie on back, knees bent, one hand on chest, one on belly
**Cues:**
- Inhale through nose, belly rises
- Exhale through mouth, gentle pelvic floor lift
- Feel ribs expand laterally
- No breath holding
- Coordinate breath with pelvic floor
**Avoid:** Chest breathing only, forcing the contraction, holding breath

---

### 59. Diaphragmatic Breathing
**Setup:** Supine or seated, hands on lower ribs
**Cues:**
- Breathe into hands - ribs expand
- 360° expansion of ribcage
- Slow exhale, ribs come in
- Keep shoulders relaxed
- 4 counts in, 6 counts out
**Avoid:** Shallow breathing, shoulder elevation, forcing

---

### 60. Pelvic Floor Activation
**Setup:** Seated or supine, relaxed position
**Cues:**
- Imagine stopping urine flow
- Lift and squeeze gently
- Hold 3-5 seconds
- Fully relax between reps
- Breathe normally throughout
**Avoid:** Holding breath, squeezing glutes, bearing down

---

### 61. Deep Squat Hold
**Setup:** Feet wider than hips, toes out, hold support if needed
**Cues:**
- Sink hips between heels
- Keep heels down
- Spine neutral, chest up
- Breathe into pelvic floor
- Hold 30-60 seconds
**Avoid:** Rounding back, heels lifting, holding breath

---

### 62. Happy Baby Stretch
**Setup:** Lie on back, grab feet or shins, knees wide
**Cues:**
- Pull knees toward armpits
- Keep sacrum on floor
- Rock gently side to side
- Breathe into stretch
- Relax pelvic floor
**Avoid:** Forcing knees down, lifting head, tensing up

---

### 63. Pelvic Tilts
**Setup:** Supine, knees bent, feet flat, arms at sides
**Cues:**
- Flatten low back to floor
- Tilt pelvis up gently
- Release back to neutral
- Coordinate with exhale
- Small, controlled movement
**Avoid:** Using glutes excessively, big movements, holding breath

---

### 64. Bridge with Ball Squeeze
**Setup:** Supine, knees bent, small ball between knees
**Cues:**
- Squeeze ball gently
- Lift hips to bridge
- Maintain squeeze throughout
- Exhale on lift
- Lower with control
**Avoid:** Over-squeezing, arching back, losing ball squeeze

---

### 65. Clamshells
**Setup:** Side lying, knees bent 90°, feet together
**Cues:**
- Open top knee, keep feet together
- Don't rotate pelvis
- Squeeze glute at top
- Control the close
- Keep core engaged
**Avoid:** Rolling hips back, using momentum, lifting feet

---

### 66. Bird Dog (Modified)
**Setup:** Hands under shoulders, knees under hips
**Cues:**
- Extend opposite arm and leg
- Keep spine neutral
- Move slowly and controlled
- Exhale as you extend
- Hold briefly at top
**Avoid:** Arching back, rotating hips, rushing

---

### 67. Cat-Cow
**Setup:** Hands under shoulders, knees under hips
**Cues:**
- Inhale: arch back, head up (cow)
- Exhale: round back, tuck chin (cat)
- Move through full range
- Coordinate breath
- Feel spine articulate
**Avoid:** Rushing, jerky movements, holding breath

---

### 68. Squat to Stand
**Setup:** Stand feet hip-width, hinge to grab toes
**Cues:**
- Drop into deep squat
- Keep grip on toes
- Stand up, straighten legs
- Feel hamstring stretch
- Drop back to squat
**Avoid:** Rounding excessively, forcing range, rushing

# VIDEO PRODUCTION NOTES

## General Guidelines for All Videos:
1. **Camera angles:** Front, side, and 45° angles for each exercise
2. **Demonstrate:** Correct form first, then common mistakes
3. **Tempo:** Show controlled tempo (2-1-2 typical)
4. **Breathing:** Visible breathing pattern when possible
5. **Range of motion:** Full ROM clearly visible
6. **Attire:** Form-fitting clothes to see muscle engagement

## Equipment Needed:
- Barbell + plates
- Dumbbells (various weights)
- Kettlebell
- Bench (flat + incline)
- Cable machine
- Pull-up bar
- Squat rack
- Leg press machine
- Leg curl/extension machine
- Resistance bands
- Ab wheel
- Boxes/platforms (various heights)
- Small ball/pillow (for pelvic floor exercises)
- Yoga mat (for floor exercises)
